Session 13 — the term node

How a term's node page renders: the depth dial, the relationships, the way in and out.
Four working demos. Same three real glossary terms — forward-only · ho · tuner — rendered as full node pages in each.

Rendered in hosystem's frozen system only (§2 inks, §3 type, §4 step, §5 ledger + link grammar, §7 touch, §12 crumb). No gold anywhere — terms carry no state. Depths 4–5 are unauthored; every dial shows only what exists (1–3) and ends clean. Dashed boxes and the "term:" bar are study scaffolding, not part of the design. Touch a dotted term to define (never navigates); click a solid link to travel. Depth moves on ↑/↓ (or ←/→ / 1·2·3) as committed steps.
